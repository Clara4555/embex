Terminal Port

HOME ➝ Terminal Port

The strategic terminals operated by Petro Terminal Logistics are located in Kazakhstan, the United States (Houston, TX), Fujairah, Singapore, Russia, Turkey, and Rotterdam. These terminals are among the best in the world for processing, storing, and transporting crude products and petroleum. In strategically placed positions with access to major shipping routes, rail, pipeline, bunkering, and road networks, we operate in the most dependable and efficient terminals for processing chemicals, petroleum, and crude products. Petro Terminal Logistics is a versatile bunker fuel terminal located at several ports throughout the globe. At our dock and jetties, we provide a variety of amenities.

Our tank farms, some of which are subterranean and others of which are above ground, enable the efficient discharge of products into pipelines and heavy-duty oil tankers. Before oil and petroleum products are officially delivered to end buyers or further to storage facilities, we have sizable industrial amenities for their storage.

Because our tank farms are contemporary, consisting of a variety of tanks, pipes, and gantries with a higher level of automation and notable modifications to depot operations, the products that arrive at our tank farms come from refineries and are fit for distribution to buyers.
